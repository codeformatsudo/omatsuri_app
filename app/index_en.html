Location

Tokiwadaira Sakura Dori
(From the Yurinoki Dori Intersection to Goko Eki-Mae Intersection, as well as Keyaki Dori up to Seiyu)
Please look at the map for the locations of events and restrooms.
※ The festival will be held regardless of weather. Some events or stalls may be cancelled due to the weather.
  • 15-minute-walk from Yabashira Station ( Shin-Keisei Line )
    　or Shin-Yahashira Station (JR Musashino Line )
  • Right in front of Tokiwadaira Station ( Shin-Keisei Line )
  • 1-minute walk from Gokō Station ( Shin-Keisei Line )
※ No parking for cars provided. Please use public transportation.
  • Tweet
Please dispose of garbage and disposable skewers in their designated collection bins.
Thank you for your cooperation!
※ Students from Tokiwadaira, Makinohara, Matsudo Fourth, and Kanegasaku JHS will be volunteering with the clean-up effort.

Please use good manners while enjoying the Cherry Blossom Festival:
  • There is no smoking in the pedestrian areas.
  • Barbecue grills and open flames are prohibited in parks near the festival.
  • Please make sure you do not cause nuisance to residents and others. Picnic in the park only during the festival's posted 'Pedestrian Only' times. ( see times above )
  • Flying of- and photography by drones is prohibited
